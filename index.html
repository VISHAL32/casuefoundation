About us

We are the people with vision trying to make the people to be aware about their society, culture and support in their devleopment towards better future. CAUSE Foundation is a non-profit organization that helps the rural people with their volunttter work in the form of service and donations from their own pockets. We are still a growing organization and our primary focus is to education people and understand the purpose of education and through which they grow to become better citizens of the country.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to make society aware about the society, educate them about the nation and their role in the society. Our mission is to make people learn beyond the books that they teach in schools, we conduct programs to teach out of the acdemic books, educate through interactive lessons and activities.

As we spread our wings and generate funding we plan to support students with merit to progress their education.

If there are donors or supporters we shall support not just education but also for their career needs. Provide them Life skills, IT skills and many more.
